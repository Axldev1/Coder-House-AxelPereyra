George Kusunoki Miller "Joji"

Joji (real name George Kusunoki Miller) is an R&B singer, producer, and songwriter who formally began his music career in 2017 and has since garnered the love and support of a large number of people.

Despite being born in Osaka, Japan and
speaking Japanese, his repertoire of songs and
recording projects are all in English.

"Joji" before the music

Before dedicating himself to music and being
known as «Joji», he was known for
«The Filthy Frank Show»
and also for his character
«Pink Guy» for said comedy show
that was uploaded on YouTube.


The curious thing is that he managed to release a large
number of songs under the pseudonym
"Pink Guy"; However, these were not serious at all.

CLAIM

TO FAME

Harlem shake

Another thing George is credited with is creating the "Harlem Shake" trend, a viral phenomenon in 2013. He was the first to upload a video for the song titled "DO THE HARLEM SHAKE (ORIGINAL)" which quickly caught the attention of many users who decided to make their own versions of the video.

Ballads 1

In 2018, he released his debut album as "Joji" titled "BALLADS 1" which includes 12 songs, among which we can mention "Test drive", "Attention", "Yeah right", and his most listened song to date, "Slow dancing in the dark." This album reached the number one position on Billboard's R&B and hip-hop music chart.

Nectar

In 2020, his second album "Nectar" was published, which consists of 18 songs and enjoyed a good reception from fans and good positions on the Billboard charts. Among some songs included in this album, we can mention "Tick Tock", "Daylight" with Diplo, "Run", "Gimme love", and "Like you do".
